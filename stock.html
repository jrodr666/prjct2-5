Stock preparation
Last updated on 12/15/2021
Stock is the slurry created by blending wood or materials with water. When blended the result is a slurry that will be used to make paper. Stock prep is the most important part of the process becuase the stock makes up the paper and bad stock will make bad paper. The stock must alos go through a process called refining where the firbes of the raw materials are putr through shear force that will make trhe fibers more exposed to grouping to other fibers. Some paper needs chemicals to gain or enhace certain properties of the paper. Paper for carboard boxes will often use chemistry to make the fiber of the paper more hydrophobic. This process is very complex becuase alot of pipes, meters, and pumps are usuaed to ensure the process is stable and cost efficient.
